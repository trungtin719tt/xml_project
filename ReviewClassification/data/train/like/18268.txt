"Oh", said a friend, taking this novel off my shelf. "This sounds like a boring topic for a story!"She thought it was a story about collecting butterflies, as that is what the title and cover suggest. And I answered:"It is not about that at all, and it is one of the most suspenseful and scary novels I ever read!"But then I thought that it actually is about collecting butterflies after all. One just rarely thinks of the fact that you kill them and pierce them with a needle to be able to look at their beautiful wings at your leisure instead of chasing after them flying free.So the cover and title say it all, just not straightforward.I guess this book made me a strong supporter of butterflies' right to fly ...
