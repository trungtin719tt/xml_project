Island of the Blue Dolphins is an exciting tale about life alone on an island. This book has won a Newbery Award and is just fun to read. Karana got left behind with her brother. This book teaches a great lesson, and is easy to read. Island of the Blue Dolphins has great details. Scott O'dell "hit the nail on the head with this book! I recommend this book to all.After Karana and her brother got left behind something happened! Karana Survived on her own by making weapons. She kept pets so she was not lonely. The boat who took her people may never come back. Read Island of the Blue Dolphins to find out more!-ReaderX
