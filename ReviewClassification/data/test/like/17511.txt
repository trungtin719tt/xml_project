This is a shocking story on a number of levels. Aimee Cabo reveals herself to be a pillar of strength as she opens up about her horrendous life journey and her triumph over her pain. This book had an engaging story, despite being hard to read at times. Cabo's life has been filled with upsetting and traumatic obstacles, yet she managed to overcome these challenges due to the love of her husband and children and her solid religious faith. Cabo is very brave to allow herself to be so vulnerable, yet so strong to be able to share her story.
