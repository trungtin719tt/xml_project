The book Love is the Answer God is the Cure depicts the difficult life of the author Aimee Cabo Nikolov, who has been abused as a child for many years by her stepfather. Subsequently she was pitted against the whole world during the 5 years trial that was named "The case from hell" by the journalists covering it. Her own mother didn't believe her which devastated her.Despite this unimaginable suffering she survived after that a 14 years custody battle, struggle with addiction and the near killing of her daughter by a tug that rented a room from her on Craigslist.This book is an inspirational story that shows how Aimee's faith and perseverance guided her to form a loving family with the man of her dreams.I sincerely recommend this book to anybody, it is a page turner.
